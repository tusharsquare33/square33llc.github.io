About

Square 33 explores the foundations of compute infrastructure and its role in modern institutions and markets. The work centers on first-principles analysis of continuity and systemic risk in the AI era, with an emphasis on how upstream technical realities shape downstream economic and governance outcomes. The aim is to clarify what must be measured, planned, and stewarded for resilient growth.

From 2015–2024 at Intel, I worked across the Office of the CTO and Intel Federal on applied research capture and transition. Highlights include initiating and co-developing one of Gordon Moore’s final public statements (a foreword for DARPA’s Electronics Resurgence Initiative in 2017) and producing, at DARPA’s request, a Moore’s Law commemorative film for the agency’s 60th in 2018. The through-line has been connecting early silicon and systems research to practical pathways for commercial and government stakeholders.
