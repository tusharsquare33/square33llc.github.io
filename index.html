About

Leader with a background spanning applied research, commercialization, and federal strategy. At Intel (2015–2024), I served in the Office of the CTO and Intel Federal, leading cross-functional teams on DARPA programs, including work that contributed to the Electronics Resurgence Initiative.

In 2017, I initiated and co-developed one of Gordon Moore’s final public statements—a foreword for DARPA’s ERI—and in 2018, at DARPA’s request, produced a Moore’s Law commemorative film for the agency’s 60th anniversary. My work consistently linked early-stage silicon and systems research to applied transition paths across commercial and government customers.

After nearly a decade at Intel, I founded Square 33 LLC to continue exploring the future of compute infrastructure and AI resilience. My current work centers on frameworks that clarify how compute infrastructure grounds institutions and markets.
